FAQ

  • Why do you need my email address?

    Due to the nature of online services we need a proven method of communicating order confirmation and caller notifications. Email gives us that best option.

  • Can I order a Pipes Number without email?

    Contact us. We'll see what we can do

  • Do I get refunded for unused minutes?

    No, the Pipes Number you receive is reserved for a period of 3 weeks with a total of 20 minutes talk time.

  • What happens if someone calls me and didn't leave a message?

    We send out an email after every phone call so you know someone has tried to call you.

  • Can I call people with my Pipes Number?

    Yes, call your Pipes Number and follow the voice prompts.

  • Do you provide voice mail?

    No, we route (send) the call to your phone.

  • Do you record the calls?

    Hell no

  • Can people find out my real number?

    Yes, If you have your phone number as part of your voice mail prompt or you call them.

  • Will I continue to get calls once the Pipes Number has expired?

    No, once the time has expired all calls are dropped and you will receive emails up to 48 hours after expiration letting you know when someone has called (with the chance to re-purchase the number)

  • Sometimes I get a number that is long distance why?

    We attempt to match a phone number with your calling area, but it's not always possible to do so.
